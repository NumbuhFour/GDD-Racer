Story: 
You are a member of the infamous Lakota mafia, and part of the
business is selling contraband peace pipes to the warring factions of 
the city. Times are getting tough, and as the premier delivery boy for
the Lakota mafia, it's your duty to deliver the peace pipes to the 
dropoff points. Beware! Police have caught onto the operation and are
looking to catch you red-handed with the contraband!

Objective: Evade the police and make it to the three drop-off points
without taking too much damage to your car

Level Design:
Currently have one level that several city blocks, with alleyways wide
and narrow that the player can navigate. Drop off points are in 3 of
the alleyways.
